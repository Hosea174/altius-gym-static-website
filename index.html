Our services

A Diverse range of classes

Get fit and have fun with our diverse range of gym classes, taught by experienced coaches. With over 10 classes to choose from, including popular options like Zumba, Yoga, and HIIT, there's always something for everyone. Join us and discover your new favorite way to workout!

Modern gym equipment

Our gym is equipped with state-of-the-art machines and equipment that are designed to help you achieve your fitness goals efficiently and effectively. From cardio machines to weightlifting equipment, our gym has everything you need to push your limits and challenge yourself every day.

Post-workout bliss

Relax and rejuvenate after your workout with our luxurious spa services. We offer steam rooms, sauna, hot baths, and massages to help you unwind and soothe your muscles. Pamper yourself and enjoy the ultimate post-workout experience at our gym.

About us

Altius gym

#1 gym in Ethiopia

Altius Gym was founded with the vision of creating a fitness community that inspires individuals to reach their highest potential. We believe that fitness is not just about physical strength, but also about mental and emotional well-being. That's why our team of experienced trainers and staff are dedicated to providing our members with a welcoming and supportive environment that fosters growth and achievement. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete, we're here to help you achieve your goals and become the best version of yourself
